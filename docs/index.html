⬡ Gesture RC Car

Control a real RC car with hand gestures.
No apps. No wearables. Just your hand.

MediaPipe · Random Forest · ESP8266 · WebSocket

How to Control the Car

1

Power on the car

ESP8266 starts broadcasting WiFi automatically
2

Connect phone WiFi to
GestureCar

Open network — no password needed
3

Open in phone browser
http://192.168.4.1

The control app loads directly from the car
4

Tap Start → show hand to front camera

Closing the browser tab stops the car automatically

Gestures

FORWARD
Open palm facing camera
STOP
Closed fist
LEFT
Index pointing left
RIGHT
Index pointing right
REVERSE
Thumbs down
